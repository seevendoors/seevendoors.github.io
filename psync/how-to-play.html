Game Overview

It is not competative, its collabarative !!! Dive into Psync, a pulse-pounding card game that tests the limits of teamwork and non-verbal communication.

Core Concept: The lower a card is, the earlier it should be played. A 5 would be played quickly, while an 80 would be held longer.
Key to Success: Over time, players become synchronized in their sense of timing, developing a collective feeling for the right moment to play each card.

Gameplay Rules

‚ñº

Starting Each Level

1. Each player places one hand palm down on the table to indicate readiness

2. When all are ready, remove hands and begin

3. Players can call "stop" at any time to refocus the team

Critical Rules:
  • No communication about card values
  • No secret signals
  • No taking turns - play when you believe you have the lowest card
  • Cards must be played one at a time in ascending order

Errors & Penalties

‚ñº

If a card is played out of sequence:

  1. Game is immediately halted
  2. Team loses one life
  3. All cards lower than the played card must be discarded
  4. Team refocuses and continues the current level

Special Actions & Rewards

‚ñº

Throwing Stars

Players can use a throwing star to help the team:

  • Any player can suggest using a star by raising their hand
  • If all agree, each player discards their lowest card
  • Discard one throwing star token

Level Rewards

Teams receive rewards for completing certain levels:

  • Level 2: +1 throwing star
  • Levels 3, 5, 6, 8, 9: Additional rewards as shown on level cards

Victory & Challenge Mode

‚ñº

Victory Conditions

  • Complete all levels successfully
  • Maintain at least one life token
Challenge Mode:

After winning, continue playing in blind mode:

  • Start at level 1 with remaining lives and stars
  • Play cards face down
  • Check sequence at the end of each level
  • Lose one life for any mistakes
