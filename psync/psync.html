Game Setup

Start with a deck of 100 numbered cards (1-100). Deal cards based on the current level number.

2 Players

  • Levels 1-12
  • 2 Lives
  • 1 Throwing Star

3 Players

  • Levels 1-10
  • 3 Lives
  • 1 Throwing Star

4 Players

  • Levels 1-8
  • 4 Lives
  • 1 Throwing Star

Gameplay Rules

Core Rules

  • Players must place cards in ascending order Cards must be played from lowest to highest number
  • No turns - play when ready Play your card when you think it's the lowest remaining
  • No sharing information or signals
  • Focus together as a team
